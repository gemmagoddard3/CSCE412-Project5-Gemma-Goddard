He Before Me.

He Before Me is a Christian Small Business I officially opened in January 2023. The mission of He Before Me is to let the goodness of God be known to all. He Before Me sells t-shirts, crewnecks, stickers, and digital prints. Opening and operating my business has been a challenging endeavor, but it sparked my interest in website design. I have gained an appreciation for understanding user needs and thinking in the eyes of the user. I have had three website iterations, and am continually trying to improve it to optimize the user's interaction with my website. I use a third-party service to build and operate my website, but I aspire to enhance and personalize my website more with increased knowledge in HTML and CSS learned in this class.

Light It Up Electic.

This past summer, I partnered with a San Antonio-based construction company to design a webpage highlighting their past projects and displaying available job openings. This project involved client meetings to comprehend their requirements and follow the UX design process learned in my Google UX design certification course to develop an efficient website. The project is currently in progress but is anticipated to be finished soon. Similar to my small business, this project was developed using a third-party application. However, I aim to utilize the insights gained from this course to enhance this project.

Future Project.

A project that I hope to start soon is an app aimed at optimizing the process of creating a grocery list. My motivation behind this project was the inconvenience of time creating a grocery list for meals I cook frequently. The functionality of this app would entail uploading your recipes to the app through a picture or pdf, and each week the user would choose which meals they want to make, and the app would generate the list for them. I seek to apply the skills I learn this year to develop this app and hopefully release it to others.
